DevelopND

Bridging the gaps between communities' awareness, participation and partnership with the laudable strides and development goals of the Government for the great people of the Niger Delta..

About

The Develop Niger Delta is an initiative created for the purpose of ensuring transparency, engendering participation and involvement of all within the Niger Delta States in the development goals and strides in the different governmental agencies, establishments and the relevant stakeholders given the mandate to bring about these developments to the people.

- Our Vision -

To bring about sustainable development and quality service delivery in the Niger Delta
